Frequently asked questions

How can I use my credits?

Credits are used to pay for your usage of CI/CD pipelines and you can spend them in any combination of instances.

Do credits expire?

No, your credits don't expire, however unused credits will be forfeited if you cancel your subscription.

Can I change my instance once selected?

Yes, you can change your configuration at any time.

What if I need a different instance or platform?

We plan to keep expanding our offerings with ARM, GPU, more hardware specs and macOS in the near future. Drop us a line if you have more specific needs.

What happens at the end of my free trial?

Your accound will be upgraded to the Starter plan and charged accordingly.

Is it possible to stay in my plan but increase some limits?

Yes. You may use addons to increase: contributors, storage and data transfer.

Will my storage allowance expire?

No, your storage allowance doesn't expire.

Will my data-trasnfer allowance expire?

Yes, your data-transfer allowance is reset at the begining of your billing cycle.
