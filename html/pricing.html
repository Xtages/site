Frequently asked questions

What happens at the end of my free trial?

Your account will be upgraded to the Starter plan and charged accordingly.

Is it possible to stay in my plan but increase some limits?

Yes. You may purchase addons to increase: storage, compute power and CI/CD minutes. Feel free to contact us for more information.

Do CI/CD minutes expire?

Yes, your minute allowance is reset at the beginning of your billing cycle.

Can I change my CI/CD runners once selected?

We currently only support one type of runner (Linux) but in the future you'll be able to select the spec of the runners used in your pipelines.

What if I need a different instance or platform?

We can accommodate most needs for DBs and app server specs.

We plan to keep expanding our CI/CD platform offerings with ARM, GPU, Windows and macOS in the near future.

Drop us a line if you have more specific needs.

Will my data-transfer allowance expire?

Yes, your data-transfer allowance is reset at the beginning of your billing cycle.
